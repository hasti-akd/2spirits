2nd Annual 2-SPIRIT POWWOW

Join us on Saturday, May 27, 2023 for Toronto’s 2nd Annual 2-Spirit Powwow, hosted by 2-Spirited People of the 1st Nations. Please come with a good heart and mind. This is a safe and welcoming space for everyone.


Date: March 27, 2023

Time: 10:00 AM To 6:00 PM

Location: Downsview Park, 15 Carl Hall Rd, Toronto, ON, M3K 2E2

Harm Reduction Drop In Every Friday

Every Friday, 2-Spirited People of the 1st Nations will be offering a drop-in style hot meal where people will be given access to Harm Reduction materials, food, clothing (by donation only) and a place to get away from the cold!


Date: Every Friday Starting March 10, 2023

Time: 12:00 To 3:00 PM

Location: 145 Front Street East Unit 105

Grief Support Through Art Therapy

2-Spirited People is now offering a Grief Support Group using Art Therapy. Art therapy facilitates self exploration and understanding by using the creative process with critical thinking and problem solving skills to use imagery, colour and shape to articulate thoughts and feelings that may be difficult to express through words. There will be options to paint, sketch, colour,…


Date: Every Wednesday Starting March 15, 2023

Time: 11:30 AM To 2:30 PM

Location: 145 Front Street East Unit 105
