Grief Support Through Art Therapy

When and Where

Wednesday, March 15, 2023

11:30 AM To 2:30 PM

145 Front Street East Unit 105

About this event

2-Spirited People is now offering a Grief Support Group using Art Therapy.

Art therapy facilitates self exploration and understanding by using the creative process with critical thinking and problem solving skills to use imagery, colour and shape to articulate thoughts and feelings that may be difficult to express through words.There will be options to paint, sketch, colour, create poetry or any other form of art you believe is the best for you to express your story. For more information contact support@2spirits.org or to sign up please use the link below.
